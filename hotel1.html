Marrakesh, a former imperial city in western Morocco,
is a major economic center and home to mosques, palaces and gardens.
The medina is a densely packed, walled medieval city dating
to the Berber Empire, with mazelike alleys where thriving souks (marketplaces) sell traditional textiles, pottery and jewelry. A symbol of the city, and visible for miles,
is the Moorish minaret of 12th-century Koutoubia Mosque.
