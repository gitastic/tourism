Atlas Mountains Day Tour from Marrakech Explore The Berber Village & Waterfalls

Instant confirmation

On this tour, you will be able to explore the Atlas Mountains with a professional guide. After getting picked up from your hotel, you will be driven to the High Atlas Mountains. On the way, you will pass through Tahnaout, where you will stop at an Argan Oil Co-operative to learn the production of various Argan oil-based items.


Then, you will continue towards Asni, where you will visit a traditional Berber souk and market (Saturdays and Tuesdays). You will pass through some waterfalls and the hamlets of Ait Mizan. Next, you will stop in Imlil to soak in views of ancient irrigated terrace fields of barley, corn and vegetables.


You will then travel down through the village of Ait Souka and relish a traditional Moroccan meal in a Berber house. Here, you will get a chance to go on a camel ride, before heading back to Marrakech via Moulay Gorges.
